Gmail dot-variant checker for defensive review, search, and reporting

This static tool helps Robinhood users generate the Gmail dot variants that still route to the same inbox, so they can review suspicious lookalike addresses, search their records, or report variants involved in phishing attacks. It runs entirely in your browser and does not create accounts, generate deep links, or automate signup.

What happened
Attackers reportedly combined Gmail dot aliases with a flaw in Robinhood’s account-creation email flow to make phishing emails appear more legitimate.
Why this helps
You can generate all dotted Gmail variants for your address, then search, compare, and report any lookalike addresses tied to suspicious messages.
Official guidance
Use only official Robinhood support, deny unexpected device approvals, change your password, and remove devices you do not recognize.

Generate Gmail dot variants

Enter a @gmail.com or @googlemail.com address. The tool strips dots from the local part, then generates every possible dot placement that Gmail still routes to the same mailbox.

About this phishing attack

On April 27–28, 2026, multiple reports said attackers abused a Robinhood onboarding/account-creation flow and Gmail’s “dots don’t matter” behavior to get malicious content into emails that appeared to come from noreply@robinhood.com. The goal was to trick recipients into clicking a phishing link and entering credentials or 2FA information.

Defensive use only: this page is intended to help legitimate users enumerate inbox-equivalent Gmail variants for review, search, and reporting. It does not automate anything against Robinhood or any other service.

What Robinhood says to do

  • Use only official support channels inside the app or at robinhood.com/contact.
  • If you get an unexpected device approval, select Deny.
  • Immediately change your password.
  • Review devices and remove any you don’t recognize.
  • Report suspected phishing to reportphishing@robinhood.com.
Tip: after you generate the list, use Copy all or Download CSV to keep a local reference for support/reporting workflows.
